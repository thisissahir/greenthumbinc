# Green Thumb Landscaping & Garden Center

Static marketing site for Green Thumb Landscaping and Garden Center — Dubois County's oldest florist & garden center (Jasper, IN) and most trusted landscaping company (St. Anthony, IN).

Single self-contained `index.html` (inline CSS/JS) plus brand imagery in `images/`. Styled to the Green Thumb brand book: Oswald + Montserrat type; Jade `#008E4D`, Brunswick `#126A47`, Obsidian `#101218`, White.
