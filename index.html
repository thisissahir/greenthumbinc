Jasper · Garden Center & Florist

Dubois County's Oldest Florist & Garden Center in Jasper, IN

Welcome to our Jasper garden center where green thumbs meet modern vibes! We are not your typical garden center. We blend the timeless joy of gardening with a fresh, young-at-heart approach that speaks to today's plant enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just discovering your love for all things green, we're here to inspire and empower you on your botanical journey.

Our passion for plants goes beyond just selling them. We curate a handpicked selection of the trendiest plants and gardening accessories to turn your space into an urban jungle that's as stylish as it is soothing. From succulents to statement houseplants, each one is chosen with care to bring a touch of nature's beauty into your life.

But we're more than just a full service garden center — we are a gift shop & create unique floral arrangements. Whether you're looking for expert advice or DIY planting tips, our friendly team is here to support you every step of the way.

Our primary Garden Center, located at 702 3rd Avenue in Jasper, is a treasure trove of delights for garden enthusiasts. In addition to our landscaping offerings, our Jasper location is home to a charming gift shop and florist department. From beautiful floral arrangements for all occasions to unique gifts that delight your loved ones, we have something for everyone. Need to spruce up your garden? Look no further — we offer a wide selection of plants, seeds, fertilizers, chemicals, and soils to help your garden thrive. Explore our collection of trees, shrubs, perennials, and blooming annual flowers, and don't miss out on our fresh-cut Christmas trees in December. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply looking to beautify your outdoor space, Green Thumb Jasper is your destination of choice.

Voted 'Best in Dubois County' in 2022 for our landscaping design and build services. Welcome to the landscaping side of Green Thumb, where landscaping meets lifestyle in the most vibrant way possible!

At our St. Anthony landscaping division we're not just about creating beautiful outdoor spaces – we're redefining what it means to love where you live. Our team of passionate landscapers and designers are here to turn your outdoor dreams into reality, all while keeping things fresh and contemporary.

Whether you're looking to transform your backyard into an oasis of calm or create a lively urban garden on your rooftop, we've got the expertise and the enthusiasm to make it happen. We specialize in crafting spaces that not only look stunning but also reflect your unique personality and style.

Are you a do-it-yourself kind of person? Then the St. Anthony location of Green Thumb has what you need. Our bins are full of mulch, landscaping rock, sand to help the weekend warrior in you. Visit to view our landscaping plants, boulder selection and hardscape products.

But it's not just about the products – it's about the people. We're a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for green spaces and a zest for life. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or a newbie with a vision, we're here to support you every step of the way with expert advice, creative ideas, and a whole lot of enthusiasm.
